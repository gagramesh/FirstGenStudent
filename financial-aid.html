FAFSA Basics

FAFSA stands for Free Application for Federal Student Aid. It determines your eligibility for grants, loans, and work-study programs.

  • Apply every year at studentaid.gov
  • Use your FSA ID to sign in
  • Submit as early as October 1
  • Don’t guess — use the IRS Data Retrieval Tool

Types of Aid

  • Grants: Free money you don’t have to repay (e.g., Pell Grant)
  • Work-Study: Part-time jobs funded by the government
  • Loans: Borrowed money you must pay back (see loan page for details)

Tips for Success

  • Meet your school’s priority deadline
  • Ask your financial aid office questions — that’s what they’re there for
  • Check your email — aid info often goes there first
