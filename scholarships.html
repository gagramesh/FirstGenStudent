Tips to Win More Scholarships

  • Apply early and often, don’t wait for deadlines
  • Write one great essay and tweak it for different applications
  • Apply for local or niche scholarships with fewer applicants
  • Don’t ignore small awards, they add up fast

Essays and Recommendations

Use your personal story. Talk about being a first-gen student, your goals, and your drive. Ask professors or advisors for letters early and give them plenty of context.
