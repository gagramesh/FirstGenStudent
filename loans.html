Types of Loans

  • Subsidized: Government pays interest while you’re in school
  • Unsubsidized: Interest builds up from day one
  • PLUS Loans: For parents or grad students; credit check required
  • Private Loans: Offered by banks, usually with higher interest

Smart Borrowing Tips

  • Borrow only what you absolutely need
  • Use subsidized loans first, if available
  • Understand the repayment terms before accepting

After Graduation

You'll have a 6-month grace period after graduation before repayment begins. During this time, set up a budget and choose your repayment plan:

  • Standard (10 years)
  • Graduated (starts small, increases over time)
  • Income-Driven (based on what you earn)
